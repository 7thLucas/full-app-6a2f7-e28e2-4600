# Veroflower — Flower Store Landing Page

## What It Is
A simple, elegant two-section landing page for a local flower store named Veroflower. Clean, minimal web presence to showcase flowers and drive walk-in visits.

## Audience
Walk-in customers — locals who discover Veroflower online and choose to visit the store in person.

## Positioning
Premium yet approachable local florist. Freshness, craft, and the joy of gifting flowers — communicated in seconds.

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: Veroflower
- **Tone**: Warm, elegant, feminine, inviting
- **Palette**: Blush pink, soft green, white
- **Voice**: Simple, poetic, uplifting, minimal

## Product Scope — Two Sections Only
1. **Hero Section** — Store name "Veroflower", bold floral headline, short tagline, primary CTA ("Visit Us"). Beautiful flower showcase imagery.
2. **Store Info Section** — Store location address and business hours for walk-in customers.

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity first — nothing extraneous
- Visual-led — flowers speak louder than text
- Every element drives walk-in foot traffic via the "Visit Us" CTA
