The Problem

Local florists are invisible online — before a customer even leaves home

Walk-in customers decide where to go before stepping out the door. Without a web presence, Veroflower loses foot traffic to whoever appears first — regardless of quality.

The Solution

A focused landing page that converts curiosity into a store visit

Two sections — a bold hero with a "Visit Us" CTA and a store info section with location and hours — that communicate Veroflower's warmth and craft in seconds, and funnel every visitor toward walking in.
