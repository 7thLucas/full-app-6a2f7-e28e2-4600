Foundation
The Problem

Local flower shops are invisible online — customers can't find hours, location, or what's available.

Walk-in customers decide where to go before they leave home. Without a web presence, Veroflower loses foot traffic to whoever appears first — regardless of quality or craft.

The Solution

A two-section landing page: a hero that captures attention, and a store info section with everything needed to visit.

Section 1 Hero + "Visit Us" CTA
Section 2 Address, Hours, Contact
