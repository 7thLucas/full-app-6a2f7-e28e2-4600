# Veroflower — Flower Store Landing Page

## What It Is
A simple, elegant two-section landing page for a local flower store. Clean web presence to showcase flowers and drive walk-in visits.

## Audience
Walk-in customers — locals who discover Veroflower online and choose to visit the store in person.

## Positioning
Premium yet approachable local florist. Freshness, craft, and the joy of gifting — communicated in seconds.

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: Veroflower
- **Tone**: Warm, elegant, inviting
- **Palette**: Blush pink, soft green, warm white (#FFFAF9)
- **Voice**: Simple, poetic, uplifting

## Product Scope — Two Sections Only
1. **Hero Section** — Store name, bold floral headline ("Fresh Flowers, Crafted with Love"), short tagline, primary CTA ("Visit Us").
2. **Store Info Section** — Location, phone, email, and opening hours — everything a walk-in customer needs to find and visit the store.

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity first — nothing extraneous
- Visual-led — flowers speak louder than text
- Every element drives toward a store visit
